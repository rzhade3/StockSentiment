Sentiment Analysis

Introduction

This web page is an experiment in D3.js, and to determine whether the headlines of several popular newspapers are a good metric for measuring stock market performance.

Method

Using the Headlines API on NewsApi.org . I got the headlines from Bloomberg, Business Insider, the Financial Times, Fortune, the Economist, and the Wall Street Journal, all the business oriented news institutions that were available through the API. I then ran these headlines through the Python sentiment analysis library TextBlob, and calculated a simple average of the sentiments of the headlines for the day. These values were then correlated with the percentage change of the market for the day.
